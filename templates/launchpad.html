What's a picfic?


Picfics are serialized, illustrated novels with chapters released weekly. Every picfic encourages a community where readers can interact within the stories by uploading their own art.

PicFic is an online publishing platform that serializes stories for readers to illustrate and interact with. We're rediscovering classics, but we'll be releasing science fiction and fantasy stories in the near-future.

We're a team of writers, artists, and developers from MIT. Our mission is to redefine reading as a truly social activity befitting the digital age.
